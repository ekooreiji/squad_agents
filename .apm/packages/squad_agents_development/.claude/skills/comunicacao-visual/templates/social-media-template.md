## Best Practices

| Platform | Aspect Ratio | Text Limit |
|----------|-------------|-----------|
| Instagram | 1:1 (square) | 2200 chars |
| Story | 9:16 | - |
| Facebook | 1.91:1 | 63,206 chars |
| LinkedIn | 1.91:1 | 3000 chars |
| Twitter/X | 16:9 | 280 chars |
