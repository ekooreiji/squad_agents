## Research: E-commerce Checkout Flow

### Business Context

- **Product**: E-commerce mobile app
- **Problem**: High cart abandonment rate (72%)
- **Goal**: Understand friction points in checkout

---

## Research Plan

### Objectives

1. Identify where users drop off in checkout
2. Understand why users abandon carts
3. Validate proposed solutions

### Research Questions

1. How do users expect to complete checkout?
2. What information do they consider essential?
3. What causes trust issues during payment?

### Methods

| Method | When | Participants |
|--------|------|--------------|
| Heuristic evaluation | First week | 3 UX experts |
| User interviews | Weeks 2-3 | 8 users |
| Analytics review | Ongoing | N/A |

---

## Participant Recruitment

### Criteria

- **Include**: Purchased online in last 3 months, 25-45 years old
- **Exclude**: Never used mobile commerce

---

## Interviews

### Key Findings

#### Finding 1: Address Entry Friction

> "I hate typing addresses. I'd rather pick from saved locations or map."
> — P4

**Impact**: 6/8 mentioned address entry as painful

#### Finding 2: Hidden Costs

> "I want to see total including shipping before I commit."
> — P2

**Impact**: 7/8 abandoned carts when shipping added at end

#### Finding 3: Payment Trust

> "I need to know my payment info is secure. Logos help."
> — P7

**Impact**: 5/8 look for security badges

#### Finding 4: Guest Checkout

> "I don't want to create account just to buy once."
> — P1

**Impact**: 8/8 prefer guest checkout option

## Recommendations

| # | Recommendation | Priority | Effort |
|---|--------------|----------|--------|
| 1 | Enable address autocomplete | High | Medium |
| 2 | Show shipping early | High | Low |
| 3 | Add trust badges | Medium | Low |
| 4 | Guest checkout default | High | Low |
| 5 | Auto-save cart | Medium | Medium |
